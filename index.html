FAQ

Everything you need to know about Posture Pro.

What is Posture Pro?
Posture Pro is a smart, adjustable cushion that detects poor posture through pressure sensors and automatically adjusts using air-socket technology. It connects to a mobile app for real-time posture insights and allows both manual and auto adjustment modes.
How does Posture Pro work?
Posture Pro uses pressure sensors to detect your sitting position and posture. When poor posture is detected, it automatically adjusts the cushion support using air bladders and a micro pump system. The companion app provides real-time feedback and personalized insights.
Can I track my progress over time?
Yes! The Posture Pro app tracks your sitting duration, posture status, and improvements over time. You can view detailed analytics and see how your posture improves with consistent use.
Is Posture Pro portable?
Absolutely! Unlike ergonomic chairs, Posture Pro is lightweight and portable. You can use it at your office, home, in your car, or anywhere you sit for extended periods.
Who is Posture Pro for?
Posture Pro is perfect for office workers, students, lecturers, freelancers, gamers, streamers, and anyone who experiences back pain or wants to improve their posture while sitting.
